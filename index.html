Our History

Founded in 1956.
Family Owned Ever Since.

Robert Freson — U.S. Army Veteran of the Korean War — established HeatCool Service Co in 1956 on a simple premise: there are few things in life more frustrating than being uncomfortable in your own home.

Throughout our history, HeatCool has remained a premier, family-owned and operated heating and cooling company. We serve residential and light commercial customers with the highest level of reliability — both in the Bryant brand we offer and the quality of service we provide.

Robert was dedicated to honesty, integrity, and thoroughness. This is evidenced by the many original clients — and now their children — who continue to rely on HeatCool for their security and comfort.
