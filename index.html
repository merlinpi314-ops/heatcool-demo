Our History

Founded in 1956.
Family Owned Ever Since.

Robert Freson — U.S. Army Veteran of the Korean War — established HeatCool Service Co in 1956 on a simple premise: there are few things more frustrating than being uncomfortable in your own home.

Throughout our history, HeatCool has remained a premier, family-owned and operated heating and cooling company serving residential and light commercial customers with the highest level of reliability.

Robert was dedicated to honesty, integrity, and thoroughness — values that still define HeatCool today. Many of our original clients, and now their children, continue to rely on us for comfort and security.
