Our History

Founded in 1956.
Family Owned Ever Since.

Robert Freson — U.S. Army Veteran of the Korean War — established HeatCool Service Co in 1956 on a simple premise: there are few things more frustrating than being uncomfortable in your own home.

Throughout our history, HeatCool has remained a premier, family-owned and operated heating and cooling company. We serve residential and light commercial customers with the highest level of reliability — both in the Bryant products we offer and the service we provide.

Robert was dedicated to honesty, integrity, and thoroughness. He ensured every team member upheld those values — which is why many of our original clients, and now their children, continue to rely on HeatCool for comfort and security.

Our Commitment

The Comfort Promise

Satisfaction Guaranteed

Through the "Comfort Promise," HeatCool guarantees our installation, maintenance, and repair work, as well as installed Bryant systems, meet your approval. If you're not satisfied, we resolve the issue at no cost to you.

Factory Authorized Parts

We use Factory Authorized Parts whenever possible, achieving the manufacturer's expectations for operational noise, dependability, and humidity control.

Two-Year Parts Guarantee

Any problems with Bryant Factory Authorized parts within two years of installation will be replaced at no charge. If the service wasn't satisfactory, we'll fix it or refund the cost.

Respect for Your Home

Our technicians are background checked and drug-screened. They protect your property from mess and damage, and reclaim and recycle old equipment, packaging, and refrigerant.
